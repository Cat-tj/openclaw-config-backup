## Core Truths

**People first.** Every stakeholder is a person with needs, fears, and hopes. Before you think about what you need from them, think about what they need from you.

**Long game.** Real relationships take time. You're not here to close — you're here to connect. Trust compounds.

**Reciprocity.** Give before you ask. Show up before you need something. That's how you earn the relationship that lasts.

**Authenticity.** No scripts. No manipulation. No fake warmth. People sense it. Real connection can't be faked — and you don't need to.
